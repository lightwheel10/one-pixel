Privacy Policy

Last updated 20 June 2026

OnePixel ("we", "us") builds websites for businesses. This policy explains what information we collect when you contact us or fill in one of our forms, how we use it, and the choices you have. If anything here is unclear, email us at hello@onepixel.in.

What we collect

When you reach out to us, through our website, a lead form, or an ad, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone or WhatsApp number
  • Your business name
  • Your answers to our questions, such as what you need, your budget range, and your timeline

How we use it

We use your details only to respond to you and help with your project. That includes recommending the right plan, sending you a quote, and contacting you about your enquiry by WhatsApp, call, or email.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your information. We use trusted services to run our business and our ads, such as Meta and Google, and your information may be processed by them for those purposes. We only share what is needed for these services to work.

Cookies and analytics

Our website and ads may use cookies and measurement tools, such as the Meta Pixel and Google Analytics, to understand how people find us and to improve our ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How long we keep it

We keep your details only as long as we need them to help with your enquiry and our ongoing relationship, then we remove them.

Your choices

You can ask us to show you, correct, or delete the information we hold about you at any time. Just email hello@onepixel.in and we will take care of it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top shows when it last changed.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email us at hello@onepixel.in.

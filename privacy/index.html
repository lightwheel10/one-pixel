Privacy Policy

Last updated 25 June 2026

OnePixel ("we", "us", "our") is a website design studio based in Noida, India. This policy explains what personal information we collect when you contact us or fill in a form on our website, how we use it, and the rights you have under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. If anything here is unclear, email us at hello@onepixel.in or call +91 63070 22880.

What we collect

When you reach out to us, through our website, our "Start a project" form, or one of our ads, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone or WhatsApp number
  • Your business name (optional)
  • Your answers to a few short questions, such as your main goal, the stage your business is at, and when you want to launch

We do not ask for sensitive information, and please do not send us any through these forms.

How we use it

We use your details only to respond to you and help with your project. That includes recommending the right plan, sending you a quote, and contacting you about your enquiry by WhatsApp, call, or email.

Your consent

We process your information based on the consent you give us when you submit a form or get in touch. You can withdraw your consent at any time by emailing hello@onepixel.in. Withdrawing it will not affect anything we did with your information before you asked us to stop.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your information. When you choose to continue on WhatsApp, your enquiry is sent through WhatsApp, which is run by Meta, so it is also handled under Meta's terms. We only share what is needed for our service to work, and we never share your details for someone else's marketing.

Cookies and the Meta Pixel

Our website uses the Meta Pixel, a measurement tool from Meta, to understand how people find us through our ads and to improve them. It may set cookies in your browser. You can block or clear cookies through your browser settings, and you can manage ad personalisation in your Meta account settings.

How long we keep it

We keep your enquiry details for up to 12 months after we last hear from you, then we delete them, unless you ask us to remove them sooner or we need to keep them to meet a legal requirement.

Keeping your information safe

When you submit a form, your details are sent over an encrypted connection (HTTPS) and stored in a secured database on our own servers, which only a small service with restricted access can write to. We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us against loss, misuse, and unauthorised access.

Your rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you can ask us to:

  • Show you the information we hold about you
  • Correct or update anything that is wrong
  • Delete your information
  • Withdraw your consent

Just email hello@onepixel.in and we will take care of it. If you are not happy with how we handle your request, you have the right to complain to the Data Protection Board of India.

Children

Our website and services are meant for businesses, not children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top shows when it last changed.

Contact

Questions about your privacy, or want to make a request about your information? Reach us at hello@onepixel.in or +91 63070 22880. OnePixel is based in Noida, India.
